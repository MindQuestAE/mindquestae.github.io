Event Details

Everything you need to know about the competition

Competition Format

The competition consists of preliminary rounds followed by Grand Finale.

  • Written Prelims
  • Category-based questions
  • Grand Finale with interactive buzzer rounds
  • Live audience participation

Eligibility Criteria

Open to all High School Students of Indian Diaspora.

  • Students in Grade 8-12 of Indian Curriculum
  • Students in Year 9-13 of British Curriculum or equivalent
  • Age between 14-18 years
  • Currently enrolled in UAE schools
  • Valid student identification required

Age Requirement: Must be a high school student at the time of registration

Important Dates

Mark your calendars for these crucial milestones.

  • Registration Opens: September 5, 2025
  • Registration Deadline: September 30, 2025
  • Preliminary Round: October 19, 2025
  • Grand Finale: October 19, 2025

Rules & Regulations

Competition guidelines and code of conduct.

  • Participation is limited to teams of two.
  • No external aids or devices allowed
  • Punctuality is mandatory
  • Respectful behavior towards all participants
  • Decisions of Quiz Master will be final
  • Fair play and integrity expected

Note: Violation of rules may result in disqualification

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the competition

What are the registration requirements?

High School Students of Indian Diaspora (Grade 8-12 of Indian Curriculum/Year 9-13 of British Curriculum or equivalent). Participants must provide valid student identification, School IDs from UAE schools.

Is there a registration fee?

This event doesn't have any registration fee.

What topics will be covered in the competition?

The competition would have general knowledge topics including, but not limited to world, India, Science & Technology, Current affairs, Sports, Literature, Arts & Entertainment

Are there prizes for winners?

Yes! Winners receive trophies, certificates, and recognition. All participants receive certificates of participation.

Can parents attend the competition?

Parents and family members are welcome to attend the finals and support the participants.
