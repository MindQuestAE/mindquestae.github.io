Event Details

Everything you need to know about the competition

Competition Format

The competition consists of preliminary rounds followed by semifinals and finals.

  • Multiple choice questions
  • Rapid-fire rounds
  • Interactive buzzer rounds
  • Category-based questions

Question Categories: Science & Technology, History & Geography, Current Affairs, Literature & Arts, Sports & Entertainment

Eligibility Criteria

Open to all Indian high school students studying in the UAE.

  • Students in Grades 9-12
  • Currently enrolled in UAE schools
  • Valid student identification required
  • Parental consent for participants under 18
  • School affiliation documentation

Age Requirement: Must be a high school student at the time of registration

Important Dates

Mark your calendars for these crucial milestones.

Rules & Regulations

Competition guidelines and code of conduct.

  • Individual participation only
  • No external aids or devices allowed
  • Punctuality is mandatory
  • Respectful behavior towards all participants
  • Decisions of judges are final
  • Fair play and integrity expected

Note: Violation of rules may result in disqualification

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the competition

What are the registration requirements?

Students must be enrolled in grades 9-12 at a UAE school, provide valid student identification, school enrollment certificate, and parental consent for minors.

Is there a registration fee?

Please contact the organizers for information about registration fees and payment procedures.

What topics will be covered in the competition?

The competition covers Science & Technology, History & Geography, Current Affairs, Literature & Arts, and Sports & Entertainment.

Are there prizes for winners?

Yes! Winners receive trophies, certificates, and recognition. All participants receive certificates of participation.

Can parents attend the competition?

Parents and family members are welcome to attend the finals and support the participants.
